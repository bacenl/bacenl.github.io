[Check out the GitHub repo here!](https://github.com/bacenl/chinese-txt-to-anki)

Python workflow to convert list of Chinese words into Anki cards.

The format of each card is specified by a prompt.

I built this for myself to help myself study Chinese. I could already recognize a good amount of characters, but wanted a better way to compartmentalize new radicals / characters in case I haven't seen them before.
