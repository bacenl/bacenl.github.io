A puzzle-platformer built for Century Games Game Jam 2024 around the theme "Into the Unknown." Explore dark caves and use fish with special abilities to light up your path forward.

[Play it on itch.io →](https://bolin8621.itch.io/cave-diver)

**Behind the build**

Built in 2 weeks for Century Games Game Jam 2024. I was responsible for game mechanics and art since I was overseas at the time and could not commit to programming. Despite that, we believed that we managed to push out a pretty polished game given the time we were given!
