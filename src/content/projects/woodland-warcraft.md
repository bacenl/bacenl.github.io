Woodland Warcraft is an online multiplayer PvE game, inspired by Rabbit and Steel and Vampire Survivors. Have fun in a fast-paced co-op game where you fight waves of enemies with friends. Pick a class, learn enemy patterns, and try not to die!

[Play it on itch.io →](https://bacen.itch.io/woodland-warcraft)

# Behind the build

Built over 3 months for NUS Orbital.

We worked with Godot's high level networking, and had to deal with RPCs, authorities, and server / clients. The program also enforced good software engineering practices, so it was a good place to learn those concepts! The two classes we made are quite fun, so do give it a shot!
